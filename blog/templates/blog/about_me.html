Hi , I am Arindam. I live in India, Pune and I am a travel enthusiast, a Pythonist, a music afficando. And more keeps adding up with time

Life and Career

I started my career as a Junior Instrument Engineer in a Paper manufacturing plant in Raipur, Chattisgarh. It was the middle of the job crisis black period year 2008 and I had no option but to make the best of what I got. I think I did well during my 11 month stay . I actually loved the hot chemical aroma filled environment of a paper machine.

I got my first break into the IT world and eventually an IT city in 2009 . This time the credit solely goes to my mother . I had already given up all hopes when my mother dragged me to a job fair where for 3 days, more than 15000 people attended it. But I was the only one to make it inside EA Mobiles. No stopping now. For two and a half year, I laboured day and night .

Few moments from those days

I got to practise the programmer inside me as a professional in 2012. I joined bigbasket.com in Bangalore. The team was small. The office smaller. Yet I loved it. Met a lot of geeks, inspiration was overflowing. I sharpened my Python and Computer Science skills and eventually arrived at Pune, Maharashtra. I had spend enough time in South India and it was time now to switch to the north . And it was well worth it . I got to take a break from the struggle that kept me awake from 6 years . I discovered , trekking, travelling , music, life .
